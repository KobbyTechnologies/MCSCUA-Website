The current Strategic Plan covers the period 2021-2025. It identifies three key issues for strategic focus. These are:

  • Sustainable sand conservation and utilization
  • Research Development and Innovation
  • Corporate and Institutional strengthening.
  • To promote sustainable sand conservation and utilization within Makueni County

The key result areas will be achieved through the following strategic objectives:

  • Sand conservation will be achieved through implementation of strategies aimed at promoting conservation while sand utilization will be achieved through regulation.
  • To Promote Research, Development, Innovation and Knowledge management on Sand Resources.
  • This will be achieved through promotion of research, development, innovation and knowledge management on sand and sand resources and making use of the research recommendations.
  • To Strengthen Corporate and Institutional Capacity
